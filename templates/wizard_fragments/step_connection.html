Plex Connection
Enter your Plex server's address and authentication token.
Use your server's LAN IP address — not localhost (won't work inside Docker).
How do I find my Plex token?
Option 1 — Plex Web:
Sign in at plex.tv → click your avatar → Account.
In the URL you'll see X-Plex-Token=XXXXXX.

Option 2 — Plex support article:
support.plex.tv/articles/204059436

Option 3 — Any Plex API request:
Open your browser dev tools, go to Network, and look for X-Plex-Token in any plex.tv or your server request headers.
